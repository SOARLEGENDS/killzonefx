About KillZone

KillZone is a lightweight, real-time forex session tracker designed for traders who want clarity — not confusion.

We focus on showing accurate market sessions, automatic daylight saving time (DST) adjustments, and clear liquidity overlaps without bloated indicators or broker bias.

KillZone does not provide trading signals or financial advice. It is built purely to help traders understand when the market is active — not what to trade.

Built for speed. Built for accuracy. Built by traders.
