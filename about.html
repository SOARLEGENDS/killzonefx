About KillZone FX

KillZone FX is a live Forex market tracker designed for traders of all levels. Our goal is to provide real-time trading session information, economic events, and actionable tips to help you make smarter trading decisions.

This site is maintained independently and is continuously updated to reflect current market conditions. We also aim to educate traders on session overlaps, liquidity, and volatility patterns.
